About Grafton Safaris

Your Gateway to African Adventures

Who We Are

We are Grafton Safaris & Tours Tanzania Limited, an international team dedicated to unveiling the mesmerizing beauty and wonder of Africa. Join us for an unforgettable safari adventure through Tanzania's most iconic parks. With us you will discover the legendary Serengeti National Park, the awe-inspiring Ngorongoro Conservation Area, and the stunning landscapes of Tarangire and Lake Manyara National Parks.

Our Adventures

For those yearning for tranquillity, we offer serene getaways to the enchanting shores of Zanzibar Island. For the true adventurers, our hiking trips offer the exhilarating challenge of conquering Mount Meru and the majestic Mount Kilimanjaro. Join us on this unforgettable journey to the peaks!

Cultural Immersion

Immerse yourself in the rich cultures of Tanzania with our unique excursions. Meet the Maasai, Hadzabe (Bushmen), Gidang'odiga (Blacksmith), Datoga, and Chaga communities and experience their vibrant traditions firsthand. For those yearning for tranquillity, we offer serene getaways to the enchanting shores of Zanzibar Island.

Our Team

Our team is a vibrant, multilingual family united by a passion for Africa's wildlife, culture, and breathtaking landscapes. We are dedicated to sharing the true essence of the African experience with the world.

The Grafton Experience

At Grafton, we excel in creating bespoke tours tailored to your preferences and budget. From the moment you contact us until we bid you farewell at the airport, we infuse every step of your journey with a personal touch, ensuring unmatched customer service and a truly unforgettable adventure.
